Welcome

Welcome to The Divine Healer's Church (TDHC)!

At TDHC, we are a Christ-centered, family-focused, non-profit Christian community committed to sharing the life-changing message of the gospel. Our mission is twofold: to faithfully preach and teach the Word of God as revealed in the Holy Bible, and to extend the love of Jesus Christ to our local community and the world.

Whether you’re just beginning your spiritual journey or seeking a deeper relationship with God, our prayer is to meet you right where you are—with no rules and no judgments. We trust that God will use us to be a vessel of encouragement, hope, and transformation in your life and the lives of those you love.

We’re glad you’re here—welcome home.
