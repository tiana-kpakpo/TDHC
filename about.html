WHY WE GIVE

Our Vision

Worry Ends When Faith Begins. The Magnificent Story of a Life-Changing Journey to God. Some phrases carry such strong comfort, such enduring consolation, that they ought to be engraved on the walls of the heart.

Community Service

To be a beacon of hope and service in our community, meeting both spiritual and practical needs through outreach programs and volunteer initiatives.

Global Impact

To extend our reach globally, supporting missions and humanitarian efforts worldwide that bring hope, healing, and the message of God's love.

Inclusive Fellowship

To create a welcoming environment where people from all walks of life can find belonging, acceptance, and spiritual growth in a diverse community.

WHAT WE ARE FOCUSED ON

Our Mission

We are committed to becoming a multi-generational, multi-ethnic, multiplying movement of Christ followers, empowering our kids and students to not only be the church of tomorrow, but the church of today.

Spiritual Growth

Fostering deep spiritual growth through biblical teaching, discipleship, and creating opportunities for meaningful worship and prayer experiences.

Compassionate Service

Serving our community with Christ's love through practical acts of kindness, meeting physical needs, and being present during times of crisis and celebration.

Next Generation

Investing in children and youth as the present and future of our church, providing age-appropriate spiritual formation and leadership development.

OUR JOURNEY

Our History

For over three decades, our church has been a beacon of hope and transformation in our community. Our rich history reflects God's faithfulness and the dedication of generations of believers.

1985

Our Humble Beginnings

Starting with just 12 members meeting in a living room, our church was founded on principles of love and community service. Pastor Justin Davies Sr. led the first service with a vision for a church that would welcome all.

1995

First Church Building

After a decade of growth, we celebrated the opening of our first dedicated church building, a milestone in our community's journey. The congregation had grown to over 200 members by this time.

2005

Community Outreach Expansion

We launched our international missions program and expanded local outreach initiatives to serve more people in need. Our food pantry, clothing ministry, and counseling services began during this period.

2015

Multi-Campus Growth

Expanding our reach, we opened our second campus in a neighboring community, bringing our message of hope and healing to more people in the region.

2020

Digital Transformation

Adapting to changing times, we embraced technology to reach more people through online services and digital ministry, connecting with thousands beyond our physical locations.
